Story Ending One

Working for my country is not an easy task, it takes blood, sweat, and tears. One thing that has kept me going were her letters that I would get often. I just keep on hoping that I would see her again.

Story Ending Two

War ended. I made it out alive. As I was taking the train back home to see my woman, a strong feeling suddenly overcomes me. I cant describe what it is but all I know was that I can't wait any longer to see her again. As the train arrived from my station, I got off and look around to see if she was there. But she was not. I got back into our small house to find the window open... Maybe she is waiting for me inside. As I walked in she was not there. But she left me a letter....She has left me. I never thought that love would hurt me more than the war.
